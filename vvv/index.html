Options Tutorial

Andrew Zhu February 18, 2025

Welcome to the world of options trading! This tutorial series demystifies options, guiding you from fundamental concepts to advanced strategies. Whether you’re a beginner or an investor seeking portfolio enhancement, we’ll cover the basics (calls, puts, terminology), explore various strategies (from simple to complex combinations), analyze the factors influencing option prices (intrinsic value, time decay, Greeks, volatility), address risks and risk management, and offer practical tips for choosing a brokerage, creating a trading plan, and finding educational resources. By the end, you’ll have a solid understanding of options, ready to navigate this dynamic market confidently and make informed investment decisions. Let’s begin!
